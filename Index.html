"The Life Of Jesus Christ"

Jesus was born to the virgin Mary in a modest village nestled in a distant corner of the world. His humble arrival embodied the aspirations and yearnings of humanity. While being the Son of God, possessing boundless wisdom and authority, He also embraced mortality, vulnerable to hunger and pain. Jesus Christ traversed the trials and tribulations of human existence completely. His intimate knowledge and perfect understanding encompass each of us individually.
Even in his youth, Jesus imparted the teachings of God. At the age of 12, He astounded those who listened to Him in the temple with His profound understanding. When Jesus commenced His ministry, He embarked on a 40-day fast in the wilderness, where He faced temptation from the devil and emerged victorious. He also underwent baptism in the Jordan River by John the Baptist. Despite being sinless, Jesus chose immersion baptism to exemplify obedience to God. Following his baptism, God proclaimed, “This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased” (Matthew 3:17), affirming Jesus's divine mission.


"The Crucifixion Of Jesus Christ"

Please watch the video!

Jesus was the Son of God. He was always kind and always chose to do right. But even though Jesus was good there were people who hated him. They did not like it when he said he was the King of the Jews. Some people hated Jesus so much that they found a way to have him put on trial and sentenced to die on the cross.


Because Jesus loved us, He was willing to sacrifice His life for us. He suffered and died on a cross because He knew that was the only way we could join Him in heaven.
